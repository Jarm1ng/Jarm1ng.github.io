About

Researcher profile

I am a researcher in medical image analysis and surgical computer vision, reading for an MSc in Advanced Computer Science at the University of Leeds and collaborating with the AI in Medicine and Surgery Group under Dr Sharib Ali.

My research centres on computer vision for image-guided liver intervention — connecting preoperative 3D anatomy to the intraoperative view through geometry-aware 3D-2D registration, anatomical landmark segmentation, and augmented-reality guidance for surgery.

More broadly, I am drawn to clinically grounded systems that carry reliable 3D understanding into the operating room.

A visibility-aware, landmark-free geometric 3D-2D registration framework for laparoscopic liver surgery, using differentiable rasterization and mask-guided self-supervision for robust alignment under severe partial visibility.
